What is SoH?

SoH (State of Health) represents the overall condition of your battery compared to its original state when new. It is expressed as a percentage. A SoH of 100% means the battery is in perfect condition, while lower percentages indicate degradation. It decreases over time due to aging, charging cycles, and environmental factors like temperature.
